GOLDEN TIP

A Traditional dish made with many spices that add amazing flavor to the rice.Made with either beef or chicken.

Ingedients

  • Medium sized onions
  • Cloves of garlic
  • Ginger
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pilau Masala
  • Oil
  • Coriander
  • Black pepper
  • Bay Leaves
  • Meat
  • Potatoes

Instructions

  1. Fry the chopped onions in the oil for a minute
  2. Add ginger and the garlic paste,let it cook for two minutes
  3. Add the meat ,season with salt and cover
  4. Allow to cook untill dry,add in the potatoes
  5. Add pilau masala and bay leaves
  6. Add in the washed rice,and let it cook
  7. Add six cups of water
  8. Cover with foil and let it cook till dry
  9. Serve hot

Accompanied with cold mango juice
